# Medford School Committee

## Agenda

Pursuant to M.G.L. c. 71 38N the Medford School Committee will convene a public hearing to members of the community interested in either for or against, in whole or in part, the Superintendent's Recommendation of the Fiscal Year 2027 Operating Budget.
